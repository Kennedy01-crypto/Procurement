About Nairobi Procurement System

A digital solution designed to streamline government procurement processes in Nairobi City County

System Overview

The Nairobi City County Procurement System is a comprehensive platform that manages the entire procurement lifecycle from Authority to Incur Expenditure (AIE) creation through to payment processing. The system ensures compliance with the Public Procurement and Asset Disposal Act while improving efficiency and transparency in county procurement operations.

Designed for use by county staff and registered suppliers, the system provides role-based access to procurement functions with automated workflows that reduce processing times and improve accountability.

Procurement Process

Standard Workflow

  1. AIE Creation: Departmental agent initiates procurement request with justification
  2. Approval: Chief Officer reviews and approves expenditure authority
  3. RLPO Preparation: Accountant verifies budget and prepares procurement documents
  4. Tendering: SCM initiates appropriate procurement method (RFQ/RFP)
  5. Evaluation: Committee evaluates bids and recommends award
  6. Contract/LPO: Issuance of formal procurement document to supplier
  7. Delivery & Payment: Goods/services delivered, inspected, and payment processed
